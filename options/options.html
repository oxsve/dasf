Advanced

Advanced and experimental settings:

Advanced settings
Pre-Scaling
If active all images are down-scaled a second time when being processed.
Tests have shown similar results while providing a minor speed up
but also sparing a lot of memory when bulk processing many large images.
Note:
We highly recommend enabling this option when experiencing huge memory usage within the batch-converter or video overlay.
Base64 Support
Base64 encoded images are not covered by the Firefox extension API.
This options solves the issue by scanning webpages for Base64 encoded images and censors them retroactively.

Video

Video censoring options:

Overlay
Automatically Overlay Videos
Creates a censored overlay over video elements.
As video frames are censored in realtime, resulting video quality will lose FPS depending on your computers performance.
Does not censor the video directly.
Recommended:
Use the context menu to create overlays on demand instead of automatically spawning overlays where they are not needed!
